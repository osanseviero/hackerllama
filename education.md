### Bachellor

I studied Computer Science at Tecnológico de Monterrey, one of the top Mexican engineering university, in Mexico City. I graduated at the top 5% of my class with a final grade of 94 over 100.

I also studied **one semester abroad in U.C. Berkeley** where I took the Foundations of Data Science and Innovation and Entrepreneurship from a social and cultural context courses. I participated in a challenge lab class that simulated the process of creating your own company. 

Some of the classes I took
* **Computer Science:** Programming Fundamentals (with C), Object-oriented Programming (C++), Data Structures, Analysis and Design of Algorithms, Computer Organization (Assembly, Arduino), Introduction to Software Engineering (Agile methodologies, UML, etc), Databases, Analysis and Modeling of Software Systems (Design Patterns), Quantitative Methods and Simulation, Compiler Design, Operating Systems, and Computer Graphics.
* **Math:** Discrete Mathematics, Differential Calculus, Integral Calculus, Probability and Statistics, Numerical Methods in Engineering, Linear Algebra.
* **Product**: Management and Business Model Innovation, Project Evaluation and Management, Management of Software Engineering Projects.
* **More practical:** Videogame Development Project, Introduction to Interactive Design, Mobile Application Development Projects.
* **Misc:** Social Responsibility and Citizenship, Electricity and Magnetism, Ethics, Humanities and Fine arts.

I also had a group-based capstone project in which we developed a website for building and hosting Machine Learning models. The main idea was that users could upload their own data, and we would do pre-processing, hyper parameter tuning, model evaluation and model deployment with a simple to use API.
